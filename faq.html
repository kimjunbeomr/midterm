Frequently Asked Questions

 Home / About US
I wish to have my wedding photo and video shoot handled by you. What is the process?

Do visit page : www.visualmakers.com/contactus

What will be the cost ?

Every wedding comprises of different customs and rituals. Hence the duration varies. Number of photographers, videographers, assistants and equipment requirements vary according to the venue, anticipated number of guests etc. We therefore submit our quotation after a brief discussion about the program.

How soon do we need to make the bookings ?

It will be on a first come first serve basis. Sometimes there will be too many inquires for a particular auspicious day, but our limit is only two wedding coverage per day.

Do I have to make an advance payment for booking ?

Yes. 25% of the agreed quotation has to be paid as an advance to reserve the date for you.

Is the advance refundable in case of cancellation ?

No. Because the date is reserved for you and we cancel all subsequent inquires

Do you accept orders from cities outside your location ?

Yes. But additional cost will be charged for travel and accommodation.

Do you shoot the bride and groom portraits before the wedding and reception?

Yes. We always seek the co-operation of the Bride and Bridegroom to be ready at least one hour before the ceremony time.

Who choose the photographs for the album?

We go through a careful process of selecting the best images from the whole shoot and this will be ready within seven working days. From this you may select the images that to be included in the album. However we wish to select the best images, design the album pages and then to know your comments, suggestions and approval to finalize the album.
